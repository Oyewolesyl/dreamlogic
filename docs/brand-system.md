# Dream Logic Brand System

Dream Logic is the final product name and must appear consistently across the application, billing, reports, admin tools, metadata, and deployment surfaces.

## Identity

The approved identity is a compact stacked wordmark:

```text
dream
LOGIC
```

The word `dream` remains lowercase, rounded, fluid, and emotionally expressive. `LOGIC` remains uppercase, structured, and more rational. The contrast between the two words is part of the product meaning: intuition and calculation, symbolism and interpretation, reflection and precision.

The supporting line is `Astrology suite`. It behaves as a removable brand signature, not interface body text and not a product tagline.

## Logo Assets

Canonical assets live in `apps/web/public/brand`:

- `fulllitelogo.svg`: full light-surface lockup.
- `fulldarklogo.svg`: full dark-surface lockup.
- `logomain.svg`: primary wordmark.
- `logoaltbrightalt.svg`: high-energy alternate.

The application uses these supplied assets rather than approximating the logo with browser fonts.

## Registered Mark

Use the registered mark only on brand lockups and export/report covers where the wordmark is acting as a brand identifier. Do not place it beside every navigation mention of Dream Logic. The mark should align optically near the upper-right shoulder of the wordmark and scale with the lockup.

## Colour

The palette is tuned around near-black foundations, warm light surfaces, midnight blue, cobalt, yellow, selective red, orange, bronze, and muted parchment. Texture is permitted inside defined brand shapes, glyphs, report covers, and selected premium surfaces; it must not become a full-interface background effect.

## Modes

Dark mode uses near-black surfaces, blue wordmark behaviour, warm accents, off-white text, and restrained borders. Light mode uses white or cream surfaces, midnight wordmark behaviour, charcoal text, and warmer accent marks. Modes are designed separately rather than inverted mechanically.

## Prohibited Direction

Do not replace the identity with moons, zodiac wheels, constellation wallpaper, glowing emblems, portraits, cosmic women, hands, eyes, generic sacred geometry, horoscope-app purple, or decorative mystical illustration. Dream Logic uses a flat modular symbolic grammar.
